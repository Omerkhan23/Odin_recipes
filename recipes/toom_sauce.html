Toom_sauce Recipe

Ingredients

  • 30 cloves of garlic
  • 2 to 3 tbs of lemon juice
  • 2 to 3 cups of any neutral oil
  • 1 tbs of salt

Directions

  1. Add 30 cloves of garlic in a food processor and mince it to really small pieces
  2. add the salt and lemon juice and imulsify them completely you will start seeing the mayo like texture
  3. first slowly add 1 cup of oil to completely imulsify it
  4. stop the blender and and scrape the sides and now add the rest of the oil slowly to completely imulsify it to gain the beautiful garlic sauce
  5. refrigirate it for future use
